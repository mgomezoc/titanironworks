Crafting Elegance in Iron Since 2010

At Titan Iron Works, we believe every entrance tells a story. Our master craftsmen blend centuries-old ironworking traditions with contemporary design to create doors that are truly one-of-a-kind.

Quality and durability have not been overlooked in any of TITAN's door systems, while we offer top-of-the-line elegance and beauty. Whether you are a builder, remodeler, or home buyer, we are excited at the opportunity to work with you.
